Get Involved

If you are a developer, scholar, or just interested in digital editions, your contributions are welcome. Join the Edirom Online community!

Development

Contribute to the codebase, suggest new features, or help with documentation on GitHub.

Community meetings

Participate in monthly community meetings to discuss progress, share ideas, and work on new features. Check dates for next meetings in the wiki. Via the edirom-l mailing list, we send invitations to the community meetings and we have Edirom related discussions on this list.

Discussion

Join the conversation, ask questions, and share your experiences via instant messaging with Matrix.

Documentation

Help improve the documentation and write tutorials to make Edirom Online accessible to a wider audience at the documentation site.

Report Issues

Found a bug or have a suggestion? Please use the issue tracker.

Edirom Summer School

In addition to the (virtual) community meetings, there is the Edirom Summer School every year in late summer at the HNI in Paderborn, where you can learn more about Edirom Online and meet other users and developers.

Meet the community at Edirom-Summer-School
