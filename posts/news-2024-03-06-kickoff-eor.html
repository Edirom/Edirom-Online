Kickoff Meeting for Edirom Online Reloaded Project

2024-03-06

The project Edirom Online Reloaded (2024–2027, funded by the DFG) had the kickoff-meeting with the whole team at 6.3.2024 in Detmold at the Musikwissenschaftliches Seminar Detmold/Paderborn, where organizational issues were discussed and the team got a first overview of the project schedule.

The project is meeting weekly to discuss the development issues and keep each other updated. In addition to this the project is hosting an Edirom-Online community meeting monthly to get the community involved in the development.
