Developer meeting with community

2024-05-15

From 13.–15.5.2024 there was an Edirom developer workshop with the edirom community developers (7 on site, 3 remote), hosted by Edirom Online Reloaded at the HNI/Paderborn University.

The workshop focused on collaborative development, sharing experiences, and planning future features for Edirom Online. Participants discussed technical challenges, exchanged ideas for improving usability, and worked together on code sprints to address current issues.

Regular developer workshops like this are essential for strengthening the Edirom community and are planned to be held again in the future.
