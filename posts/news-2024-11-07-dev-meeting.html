Developer meeting with focus on v1.0.0

2024-11-07

From 6.–7.11.2024 the edirom community had a developer meeting alongside the ViFE Klausurtagung at Hofgeismar to work on the release v1.0.0.

The meeting provided an excellent opportunity for in-depth technical discussions and collaborative planning. Developers reviewed the current status of the release, addressed open issues, and coordinated final steps for the launch of Edirom Online v1.0.0.

The close connection to the ViFE Klausurtagung enabled fruitful exchanges, strengthening the community and ensuring that the release can happen in the near future.
