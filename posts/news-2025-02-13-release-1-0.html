Release of Edirom Online v1.0.0

2025-02-13

We are pleased to announce the release of Edirom Online v1.0.0, the first official major release of the Edirom.

In a strong community effort over the past years numerous developers (often as volunteers) have contributed in fixing bugs, implementing new features and improving the documentation.

The change log for changes since the previous non-beta release v0.11.0 shows the developments.

The software development continues in the Edirom-Online GitHub repository.
