DHd 2025 poster award (3rd place)

2025-07-03

We are thrilled to announce that our poster Concurrent requirements for sustainable software (re-)development won the 3rd place at the DHd 2025 poster slam in Bielefeld! Luckily, the audience and the award committee did not take our slogan "We listen, and we don't judge" too seriously by listening closely and also judging in a very pleasant way for us. Congratulations to other award winners and to all participants, and many thanks to the DHd community and the organisers for the interesting and enjoyable conference.

Our poster is available at Zenodo: https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.14944613
