Poster at the Music Encoding Conference 2025

2025-06-06

At this year's Music Encoding Conference from 3.–6.6.2025 in London, the Edirom community presented the poster “Community-Driven Open Source Development of Edirom Online 1.0 and Beyond”.

The poster highlights the collaborative development of Edirom Online 1.0 and provides an outlook on future enhancements. The focus is on open structures, active community participation, and the sustainable promotion of open source in the field of digital music editions.

The conference offered an excellent opportunity to exchange ideas with international experts and to network with other projects. We are delighted by the great interest and positive feedback on our approach and warmly invite everyone interested to contribute to the further development of Edirom Online.
