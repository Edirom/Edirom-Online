Edirom Introduction at Edirom Summer School 2025

2025-09-09

As part of the Edirom Summer School 2025 held in Paderborn from September 8th to 12th, 2025, an introductory course on Edirom-Online took place on September 8th and 9th. Led by the Edirom Online Reloaded project team, participants received a hands-on overview of the web-based publication environment for digital music editions, exploring its core functionalities, typical fields of application, and support for scholarly-critical music editions in digital form. The course also guided attendees through the workflow of preparing data for Edirom-Online, including working with relevant encoding formats and external tools commonly used in digital editing projects.
