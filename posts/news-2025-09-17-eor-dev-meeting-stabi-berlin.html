Edirom Online Reloaded Developer Meeting in Berlin

2025-09-17

From September 15th to 17th, 2025, members of the Edirom Online Reloaded project gathered at the Staatsbibliothek zu Berlin for an intensive developer meeting. The main focus was on preparing the upcoming release, with particular attention to bug fixing, testing, and final refinements.

The meeting also provided an opportunity to discuss recent developments in the frontend and backend, align ongoing community contributions, and plan next steps for documentation and infrastructure. The Staatsbibliothek offered an excellent working environment that encouraged productive collaboration and in-depth technical exchange.
