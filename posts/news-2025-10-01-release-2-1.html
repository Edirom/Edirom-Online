Release of Edirom Online v2.1.0

2025-10-01

The new version 2.1.0 of Edirom Online is the second release with new Edirom Online architecture based on individual repositories for backend and frontend. The new version includes enhancements in the build process and the documentation. The main difference to the previous version 2.0.0 is the update to the latest backend and frontend versions which have also just been released as Edirom Online Backend v1.1.0 and Edirom Online Frontend v1.1.0. Please visit the respective releases for more information.

On Zenodo: https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.17241033
On GitHub: https://github.com/Edirom/Edirom-Online/releases/tag/v2.1.0
