Release of Edirom Online v2.2.0

2025-12-19

The release of Edirom Online version 2.2.0 marks another milestone in the projectâ€™s community-driven development. This version introduces the first integration of Web Components, including the Verovio Viewer Web Component for rendering MEI-based musical notation and a Web Component for icons. The adoption of Web Components strengthens the modular architecture of Edirom Online and facilitates reuse, extension, and experimentation beyond the core application. Alongside this architectural step, the release includes a number of fixes and refinements across frontend and backend components.
Edirom Online is developed as open-source software and thrives on active participation. Users, developers, and digital humanists are invited to explore the new release, share feedback, and contribute to future development.
